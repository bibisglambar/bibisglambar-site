✦  Bibisglambar Booking Policy

Reservation Fee

Your reservation fee is non-refundable and is applied toward your service total. It confirms your spot on the Bibisglambar calendar.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

A minimum of 48 hours notice is required. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit the reservation fee. Rescheduling within 48 hours is subject to availability and a new reservation fee may be required.

Late Arrivals

Arrivals more than 15 minutes late may result in a shortened service or cancellation. The reservation fee will not be refunded for late arrivals that result in cancellation.

No-Shows

No-call, no-show appointments will be charged the full service amount and will require full payment upfront for any future bookings.

Business Hours & After-Hours Bookings

Bibisglambar operates Thursday through Saturday, 9am – 7pm. Appointments requested outside of these hours are available upon request and subject to an after-hours booking fee. Please contact us directly to arrange.

For bridal parties, group bookings, and special events, please contact us directly for a custom quote. A larger deposit may be required to secure group bookings.

Confirmations & Reminders

You will receive a booking confirmation via email or text after your appointment is secured. Reminder notifications will be sent 48 hours and 24 hours before your appointment.
